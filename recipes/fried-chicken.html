Chicken fried rice

Description:

Fried chicken is a really easy recipe that is very flavourful. It's perfect if you want to make something quick to eat.

Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast
  • soy sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 big onions
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 500g frozen vegetables of your choice
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g boiled white rice

Steps:
  1. Brown cubed chicken breasts with soy sauce
  2. Add already boiled rice
  3. Add vegetables and frozen vegetables
  4. Scramble 2 eggs inside
  5. Enjoy!
